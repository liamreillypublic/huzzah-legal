4. The Core Experience: One Shot, One Prompt

4.1 Daily Prompt

A new prompt is published each day at a fixed UTC time. The same prompt is shown to all users worldwide. The prompt remains active until 12:00 UTC the next day; after that, the prompt is closed and you can no longer submit to it. You can submit to the current prompt at any time during its active window.

4.2 One Submission Per Prompt

You may submit only one photo per prompt. After submitting, you can see how other users interpreted the same prompt. This is the core game mechanic of Huzzah.

4.3 Retakes

You may retake your shot, which fully replaces the prior submission. Retaking deletes all data associated with the prior submission — likes, comments, views, tagged users, shares, and engagement counts. Retakes are irreversible. Retakes are available to:

  • Huzzah Pro subscribers (unlimited).
  • Users with earned free-retake credit (1 retake credit per 3 unique invited phone numbers that turn into actual Huzzah accounts).
  • Users who make a one-time in-app purchase of a single retake (product com.huzzah.retry).

4.4 Submission Visibility

At post time you choose visibility: Everyone, Friends, Followers, or Only Me. Visibility is enforced by Row-Level Security in our database. You can edit a submission's caption, location, song, tags, and visibility, but not its underlying image (use Retake instead). Submissions that have been hidden by automated moderation cannot be unhidden by you.

4.5 Tagging

The post owner may tag up to a reasonable number of other users in their submission (rate-limited to 30 tags per post per hour). Tagged users cannot self-untag through the App; if you wish to be untagged, contact the poster, block them, or report the post.

4.6 Location

You may optionally attach a coarse-grained location (city/state level) to your post. Location data is stored permanently with the submission and is visible to anyone who can see the underlying post.

4.7 Music

You may attach an Apple Music song to your post. We store song metadata (title, artist, song ID) but no streaming licence is granted; the song link opens in Apple Music when tapped.

4.8 Watermarking on External Share

When you share a submission to an external app (Instagram Stories, Snapchat, etc.), the App applies an on-device watermark (Huzzah logo, your username, prompt text) to the exported copy. Your original submission stored on our servers is not modified.

4.9 Streaks

Consecutive days of posting accumulate into a streak; missing a day resets the current streak (longest streak is preserved as a personal record). Streaks may be shown on your profile unless you hide them (or unless you are under 16, in which case streaks default to hidden).

7. Automated and Community Moderation

7.1 Automated Content Filtering

The App applies a client-side content filter on captions, comments, messages, and profile fields before sending them to our servers. Our backend applies an independent server-side filter on the same fields; the server filter cannot be bypassed by a tampered client because it is enforced as a PostgreSQL trigger that raises an exception preventing the row from being inserted.

7.2 Automated Image Moderation

Every image you submit is run through AWS Rekognition within seconds of upload. Detection thresholds and consequences are described in the Privacy Policy Section 6.3 and 8.4. CSAM-indicating images (nudity + estimated age under 18) result in immediate permanent suspension, image deletion, and NCMEC reporting per 18 U.S.C. § 2258A.

7.3 Community Reports

Users may report content and accounts. Reports filed by very new accounts are discounted (reporter must be ≥24 hours old to report posts and ≥7 days old to report accounts) to defang fresh-account brigading. Reports accumulate in rolling windows: 30 days for posts and accounts, 14 days for comments. When the count exceeds an engagement-scaled threshold, automated action follows (see Section 8.3 of the Privacy Policy).

7.4 Suspension Ladder

The first auto-issued account suspension is 1 day; the second is 7 days; the third is 30 days; the fourth and beyond is permanent. Severe violations (CSAM, terrorism, credible threats) bypass the ladder and result in immediate permanent suspension.

7.5 Effect of Suspension

While suspended, your account cannot post, comment, message, like, share, or otherwise interact with the App. Your existing content remains stored but is hidden from other users. Your profile shows as suspended. Pushes addressed to your suspended account are suppressed. On suspension expiry (for temporary suspensions), your account is automatically reinstated.
