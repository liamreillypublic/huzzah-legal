Community Guidelines

Last Updated: April 2026

Huzzah is a place for creative expression through daily photo prompts. These guidelines exist to keep our community safe, respectful, and fun for everyone. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban.

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect. Huzzah is a diverse community of people from different backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives.

  • No harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
  • No targeting individuals or groups with hateful, degrading, or threatening language
  • No unwanted sexual comments or advances
  • No mocking or shaming other users' appearances or shots
  • Constructive feedback is welcome — cruelty is not
  • Disagreements are fine — personal attacks are not

No Hate Speech

We have zero tolerance for content that promotes hatred or violence against people based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, immigration status, caste, or socioeconomic status.

This includes slurs, dehumanizing language, stereotypes, and coded hate speech. Using symbols, memes, or "jokes" to disguise hateful intent is still a violation.

Keep It Safe

  • No threats of violence or harm — even as a "joke"
  • No content glorifying, promoting, or instructing violence
  • No content promoting self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders
  • No dangerous challenges or stunts
  • No doxxing — sharing someone's private information without consent
  • No swatting, coordinated harassment, or brigading

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please contact local emergency services or a crisis helpline.

Protect Minors

The safety of young people is our highest priority.

  • You must be at least 13 years old to use Huzzah
  • No content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors
  • No solicitation of personal information from minors
  • Adults should not seek private communication with minors they don't know in real life
  • If you encounter content that exploits minors, report it immediately

Keep It Appropriate

Huzzah is a photo app for all ages (13+). Keep content appropriate for a general audience.

  • No nudity, sexual content, or pornography
  • No graphic violence, gore, or disturbing imagery
  • No content depicting or promoting illegal drug use
  • No animal cruelty or abuse

Be Authentic

  • No impersonating other people, brands, or organizations
  • No fake or bot accounts
  • One account per person — no alt accounts to evade bans
  • No artificially inflating engagement
  • No spam, repetitive content, or unsolicited promotions

Respect Intellectual Property

Only post content you have the right to share. Do not post photos taken by someone else without permission, copyrighted media you don't own, or pirated content.

Use Messaging Responsibly

  • No unsolicited explicit or sexual messages
  • No threats, harassment, or abuse via DMs
  • No spam messaging
  • Respect message privacy settings

Report, Don't Retaliate

If you see something that violates these guidelines, use the in-app report feature. Reports are anonymous. False or frivolous reports are themselves a violation.

Enforcement

Automated Moderation:

Real-time text filtering (including leetspeak and unicode evasion), image moderation at submission time, and rate limiting.

Community Reporting:

Reports are evaluated within rolling windows (30 days for posts/accounts, 14 days for comments). Thresholds scale with engagement to prevent abuse of the reporting system.

Consequences:

  • First violation: Content removed + 1-day suspension
  • Second violation: Content removed + 7-day suspension
  • Third violation: Content removed + 30-day suspension
  • Fourth violation: Permanent suspension pending review
  • Severe violations (CSAM, credible threats, terrorism): Immediate permanent suspension and report to authorities

Appeals: Contact huzzahapp@yahoo.com with your username. We respond within 7 business days. Permanently suspended accounts have 90 days to appeal.

The Spirit of Huzzah

Huzzah is about creativity, connection, and seeing the world through different perspectives. Every day brings a new prompt and a new chance to share how you see it. Be kind. Be creative. Be yourself.
