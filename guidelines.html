Community Guidelines

Last Updated: April 2026

Huzzah is a place for creative expression through daily photo prompts. These guidelines exist to keep our community safe, respectful, and fun for everyone. By using Huzzah, you agree to follow these guidelines.

Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban depending on severity and frequency.

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect. Huzzah is a diverse community of people from different backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives.

  • No harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
  • No targeting individuals or groups with hateful, degrading, or threatening language
  • No unwanted sexual comments or advances toward other users
  • No mocking, shaming, or ridiculing other users' appearances, shots, or responses
  • Constructive feedback is welcome — cruelty is not
  • Disagreements are fine — personal attacks are not

No Hate Speech

We have zero tolerance for content that promotes hatred or violence against people based on:

  • Race, ethnicity, or national origin
  • Religion or beliefs
  • Gender or gender identity
  • Sexual orientation
  • Disability or medical condition
  • Age
  • Immigration status
  • Caste or socioeconomic status

This includes slurs, dehumanizing language, stereotypes, and coded hate speech. Using symbols, memes, or "jokes" to disguise hateful intent is still a violation.

Keep It Safe

Huzzah should never be a place where people feel unsafe.

  • No threats of violence, harm, or intimidation — even as a "joke"
  • No content that glorifies, promotes, or instructs violence
  • No content promoting self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders
  • No dangerous challenges or stunts that could cause injury
  • No doxxing — sharing someone's private information (address, phone number, workplace, school) without their consent
  • No swatting, coordinated harassment campaigns, or brigading
  • If you or someone you know is in crisis, please contact local emergency services or a crisis helpline

Protect Minors

The safety of young people is our highest priority.

  • You must be at least 13 years old to use Huzzah
  • No content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors in any way
  • No solicitation of personal information from minors
  • No inappropriate contact with users known to be minors
  • Adults should not seek private communication with minors they don't know in real life
  • If you encounter content that exploits minors, report it immediately and contact the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) at CyberTipline.org

Keep It Appropriate

Huzzah is a photo app for all ages (13+). Keep content appropriate for a general audience.

  • No nudity, sexual content, or pornography
  • No sexually suggestive poses or content involving minors
  • No graphic violence, gore, or disturbing imagery
  • No content depicting or promoting illegal drug use
  • No content depicting animal cruelty or abuse
  • Artistic nudity (sculptures, paintings in museums) may be acceptable in context but is reviewed on a case-by-case basis

Be Authentic

Huzzah is about showing the world through your eyes — authentically.

  • No impersonating other people, brands, or organizations
  • No fake accounts or bot accounts
  • One account per person — no alt accounts to evade bans
  • No artificially inflating engagement (buying likes, using bots, coordinated liking)
  • No misleading content designed to deceive other users
  • No spam, repetitive content, or unsolicited promotions

Respect Intellectual Property

Only post content you have the right to share.

  • Do not post photos taken by someone else without permission
  • Do not post copyrighted images, art, or media you don't own
  • Do not use Huzzah to distribute pirated content
  • Give credit when sharing or referencing others' work
  • Respect trademarks and brand identities

Use Messaging Responsibly

Direct messages should be respectful and consensual.

  • No sending unsolicited explicit or sexual messages
  • No sending threats, harassment, or abuse via DMs
  • No spam messaging or mass unsolicited messages
  • No using DMs to circumvent blocks (via alt accounts)
  • Respect message privacy settings — if someone has DMs turned off, don't try to find other ways to contact them
  • You can only message 5 new non-friend users per day

Report, Don't Retaliate

If you see something that violates these guidelines, report it. Don't engage or escalate.

  • Use the in-app report feature (flag icon on profiles and in chats, share menu on posts)
  • Choose the most accurate report reason
  • Provide details when possible — it helps us investigate faster
  • False or frivolous reports are themselves a violation
  • Do not publicly call out or "expose" users — let the moderation system handle it
  • Reports are anonymous — the reported user will not know who reported them

How We Enforce

We use a combination of automated systems and community reports to enforce these guidelines.

Automated Moderation:

  • Real-time text filtering catches severe prohibited language (including leetspeak and unicode evasion attempts)
  • Content is checked at submission time
  • Rate limiting prevents spam and abuse

Community Reporting:

  • Reports are evaluated within rolling time windows (30 days for posts and accounts, 14 days for comments)
  • Thresholds scale with engagement — popular content needs more reports to trigger action, preventing weaponization of the report system
  • A small number of reports on a new account may trigger review faster than the same number on an established account

Consequences:

  • First violation: Content removed + 1-day account suspension
  • Second violation: Content removed + 7-day account suspension
  • Third violation: Content removed + 30-day account suspension
  • Fourth violation: Permanent suspension pending manual review
  • Severe violations (CSAM, credible threats, terrorism): Immediate permanent suspension and report to authorities

During suspension, your posts are hidden from other users and your profile is not accessible. After the suspension period, your account is restored automatically.

Appeals:

  • If you believe your content was removed or your account was suspended in error, contact us at huzzahapp@yahoo.com with your username and a description of the issue
  • We review all appeals and will respond within 7 business days
  • Permanently suspended accounts have 90 days to appeal before data is deleted

The Spirit of Huzzah

Huzzah is about creativity, connection, and seeing the world through different perspectives. Every day brings a new prompt and a new chance to share how you see it. The best Huzzah community is one where everyone feels welcome to participate, regardless of their photography skill level, background, or identity.

Be kind. Be creative. Be yourself.

These guidelines may be updated from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes within the App.
