Huzzah Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Effective Date: April 2026

Huzzah ("Huzzah," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Huzzah mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your phone number for authentication via SMS one-time password (OTP) verification. We also collect your chosen username, display name, and date of birth. Your date of birth is used solely to verify that you meet our minimum age requirement of 13 years.

Profile Information: You may optionally provide additional information including a profile photo (avatar), biography, location, education, work, personal website link, astrological sign, and interests. All profile information beyond your username is optional.

Photos and Content: When you submit a photo ("shot") to a daily prompt, we collect and store the image you capture. You may also provide a caption, tagged friends, visibility preferences, and location data associated with your submission.

Messages: When you use our direct messaging feature, we collect and store the content of messages you send and receive. Messages are stored on our servers to enable delivery and conversation history.

Music Selections: When you attach a song to your submission, we store the song title, artist name, and Apple Music catalog identifier. We do not access or store your Apple Music listening history, playlists, or library.

Reports: When you report content or users, we collect the report reason, optional description, and information about the reported content.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information: We automatically collect information about the device you use to access the App, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including pages viewed, features used, actions taken (such as likes, comments, shares, and follows), time spent on each screen, and navigation patterns.

Log Data: Our servers automatically record information when you use the App, including your IP address, access times, app features accessed, pages viewed before and after using the App, and other system activity.

Crash and Performance Data: We collect crash reports, performance metrics, and diagnostic information to identify and fix bugs and improve App performance.

Advertising Data: For users who are not subscribed to Huzzah Pro, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect device advertising identifiers (IDFA), IP address, and usage data for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We also participate in Apple's SKAdNetwork program, which allows our advertising partners to measure the effectiveness of ads without tracking your individual device.

Profile View Tracking: When you visit another user's profile, we record that visit along with your user ID, the viewed profile's user ID, the timestamp, and a view count. The profile owner may be able to see a list of users who have recently viewed their profile, including usernames and avatars. By viewing profiles, you acknowledge that your visit may be visible to the profile owner.

Security Data: For fraud detection and security purposes, we monitor for jailbroken devices, debugger attachments, app tampering, and local data manipulation. Security events are logged locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers unless suspicious activity is detected.

1.3 Information Collected from Third Parties

Phone Contacts: With your explicit permission, we access your device's contact list to help you find friends who are already using Huzzah. We normalize phone numbers (using the last 10 digits) for matching purposes only. We do not permanently store your full contact list on our servers. The normalized phone numbers are used solely for real-time matching queries and are not retained after the matching process completes. We periodically (approximately once per hour while the app is open) check whether any of your contacts have joined Huzzah and may send you a local notification when they do.

Invite Tracking: When you invite friends to Huzzah via SMS, we store the normalized phone number (last 10 digits) of the invited person along with your user ID. This is used to track successful referrals for our free retake reward program (every 3 successful invites earns 1 free retake). Invited phone numbers are retained for the duration of your account.

Apple Music: With your explicit permission, we access the Apple Music catalog through Apple's MusicKit framework to allow you to search for and attach songs to your submissions. We access song metadata (title, artist, artwork URL, preview URL) but do not access your personal Apple Music account, library, playlists, or listening history.

Location Services: With your explicit permission, we access your device's location services to allow you to tag your submissions with a geographic location. We also use location data to personalize your feed by showing nearby content. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

Photo Library: With your explicit permission, we access your photo library solely to allow you to set a profile picture. We do not scan, analyze, or upload other photos from your library.

Date of Birth: We collect your date of birth during account creation to verify you meet our minimum age requirement of 13 years. We also use it to send you an optional birthday notification. Your exact date of birth is not visible to other users.

QR Codes: The App generates a QR code containing your username that others can scan to find and follow your account. The App also uses your device camera to scan QR codes from other users. No QR code data is stored on our servers.

Photo Watermarking: When you share your submission externally (to Instagram Stories, Snapchat, TikTok, or other apps), the App adds a watermark containing the Huzzah logo, your username, and the prompt text to the exported image. This watermark is applied locally on your device and only to the exported copy — your original submission stored on our servers is not watermarked.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Providing and Maintaining the App

We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the Huzzah App, including creating and managing your account, processing your submissions, delivering messages, and providing customer support.

2.2 Personalization

We use your information to personalize your experience, including ranking and ordering content in your feed based on your social connections (friends, followers, mutual connections), engagement patterns, geographic proximity, and content preferences.

2.3 Social Features

We use your information to enable social features including friend discovery (through phone contacts and social graph analysis), friend suggestions (based on mutual connections, shared interactions, and profile views), follow notifications, and messaging.

2.4 Safety and Security

We use your information to detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, spam, security breaches, and violations of our Terms of Service. This includes automated content moderation (both client-side and server-side content filtering), report-based community moderation with engagement-scaled thresholds, rate limiting on all user actions, and account suspension for repeated violations.

2.5 Communications

We use your information to send you local notifications including: daily prompt reminders when a new prompt is available, streak reminders in the evening if you haven't posted, alerts when phone contacts join Huzzah, and birthday greetings on your birthday. You can control notification preferences in the App's settings or in iOS Settings. In-app notifications (visible in the notification bell) inform you about likes, comments, follows, reposts, shares, and tags from other users.

2.6 Advertising

For users who are not subscribed to Huzzah Pro, we display advertisements using Google AdMob. Ad serving may involve the collection of device identifiers and usage data by Google. Huzzah Pro subscribers do not see advertisements.

2.7 Analytics and Improvement

We use aggregated and anonymized usage data to understand how users interact with the App, identify areas for improvement, develop new features, and monitor overall App performance and reliability.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (username, display name, avatar, bio) is visible to other authenticated users unless you have enabled Private Account mode. Your submissions are visible to other users based on your visibility settings (Everyone, Friends, Followers, or Only Me) and Private Account status.

3.2 With Service Providers

We share your information with the following third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

Supabase: Our backend infrastructure provider. Supabase stores your profile data, submissions, messages, engagement data, and other App data. Supabase's servers are located in US-East-2 (Ohio). Supabase processes data in accordance with their Privacy Policy and Data Processing Agreement.

Google AdMob: Our advertising partner for non-Pro users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad serving and measurement. AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple: We use Apple's services for phone authentication (via Supabase Auth), Apple Music integration (MusicKit), local notifications, in-app purchases (StoreKit), and App distribution (App Store Connect).

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, in response to a valid legal process (such as a court order, subpoena, or search warrant), or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Huzzah, our users, or the public.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or other business transfer, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such transfer and any changes to this Privacy Policy.

3.5 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in US-East-2 (Ohio, United States). All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and at rest using AES-256 encryption.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) on all database tables ensuring users can only access their own data
  • Rate limiting on all write operations to prevent abuse
  • Server-side content filtering and validation
  • Secure authentication using phone-based OTP
  • Encrypted storage for photos and media
  • Automated threat detection and account suspension
  • Input sanitization to prevent injection attacks
  • Protection of sensitive profile fields from client-side tampering

4.3 Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law, typically within 72 hours of discovery.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active and as needed to provide you with the App's services.

5.2 Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time through Settings > Other > Delete Account. When you delete your account, we permanently delete:

  • Your profile and all associated information
  • All your submissions (photos, captions, songs, locations)
  • All your comments, likes, and engagement data
  • All your messages (sent and received)
  • All your friendship and follow relationships
  • All your blocks, reports, and notifications
  • All your conversation history
  • All cached profile and image data on your device

Account deletion is irreversible and typically completes within 24 hours.

5.3 Suspended Accounts

Accounts that are suspended due to community guideline violations retain their data during the suspension period. After the suspension expires, the account is restored. Permanently suspended accounts retain data for 90 days for appeals, after which data is deleted.

5.4 Anonymous and Aggregated Data

We may retain anonymized and aggregated data (which cannot be used to identify you) for analytics and improvement purposes indefinitely.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. You can view most of your data directly within the App (profile, posts, messages, settings).

6.2 Correction

You can correct or update your personal information at any time through the App's Edit Profile feature.

6.3 Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and associated data as described in Section 5.2.

6.4 Consent Withdrawal

You can withdraw consent for optional data collection at any time:

  • Contacts: Revoke in iOS Settings > Huzzah > Contacts
  • Location: Revoke in iOS Settings > Huzzah > Location
  • Apple Music: Revoke in iOS Settings > Huzzah > Media & Apple Music
  • Notifications: Revoke in iOS Settings > Huzzah > Notifications or in-app Settings > Notifications
  • Ad Tracking: Manage in iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking

6.5 Privacy Settings

The App provides the following privacy controls:

  • Private Account: When enabled, only mutual friends (users who both follow each other) can see your posts, bio, stats, and follow lists. Other users see only your username, display name, and avatar.
  • Hide Friends & Followers: When enabled, other users cannot see your friends, followers, or following lists. Counts remain visible.
  • Message Privacy: You can control who can send you direct messages (Anyone, Friends Only, or Nobody).
