Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026 · Effective Date: April 2026

Huzzah ("Huzzah," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Huzzah mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your phone number for authentication via SMS one-time password (OTP) verification. We also collect your chosen username, display name, and date of birth. Your date of birth is used solely to verify that you meet our minimum age requirement of 13 years.

Profile Information: You may optionally provide additional information including a profile photo (avatar), biography, location, education, work, personal website link, astrological sign, and interests. All profile information beyond your username is optional.

Photos and Content: When you submit a photo ("shot") to a daily prompt, we collect and store the image you capture. You may also provide a caption, tagged friends, visibility preferences, and location data associated with your submission.

Messages: When you use our direct messaging feature, we collect and store the content of messages you send and receive. Messages are stored on our servers to enable delivery and conversation history.

Music Selections: When you attach a song to your submission, we store the song title, artist name, and Apple Music catalog identifier. We do not access or store your Apple Music listening history, playlists, or library.

Reports: When you report content or users, we collect the report reason, optional description, and information about the reported content.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information: We automatically collect information about the device you use to access the App, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including pages viewed, features used, actions taken (such as likes, comments, shares, and follows), time spent on each screen, and navigation patterns.

Log Data: Our servers automatically record information when you use the App, including your IP address, access times, app features accessed, pages viewed before and after using the App, and other system activity.

Crash and Performance Data: We collect crash reports, performance metrics, and diagnostic information to identify and fix bugs and improve App performance.

Advertising Data: For users who are not subscribed to Huzzah Pro, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect device advertising identifiers (IDFA), IP address, and usage data for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We also participate in Apple's SKAdNetwork program, which allows our advertising partners to measure the effectiveness of ads without tracking your individual device.

Profile View Tracking: When you visit another user's profile, we record that visit along with your user ID, the viewed profile's user ID, the timestamp, and a view count. The profile owner may be able to see a list of users who have recently viewed their profile, including usernames and avatars. By viewing profiles, you acknowledge that your visit may be visible to the profile owner.

Security Data: For fraud detection and security purposes, we monitor for jailbroken devices, debugger attachments, app tampering, and local data manipulation. Security events are logged locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers unless suspicious activity is detected.

1.3 Information Collected from Third Parties

Phone Contacts: With your explicit permission, we access your device's contact list to help you find friends who are already using Huzzah. We normalize phone numbers (using the last 10 digits) for matching purposes only. We do not permanently store your full contact list on our servers.

Invite Tracking: When you invite friends to Huzzah via SMS, we store the normalized phone number (last 10 digits) of the invited person along with your user ID. This is used to track successful referrals for our free retake reward program (every 3 successful invites earns 1 free retake). Invited phone numbers are retained for the duration of your account.

Apple Music: With your explicit permission, we access the Apple Music catalog through Apple's MusicKit framework to allow you to search for and attach songs to your submissions. We access song metadata (title, artist, artwork URL, preview URL) but do not access your personal Apple Music account, library, playlists, or listening history.

Location Services: With your explicit permission, we access your device's location services to allow you to tag your submissions with a geographic location. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

Photo Library: With your explicit permission, we access your photo library solely to allow you to set a profile picture. We do not scan, analyze, or upload other photos from your library.

Photo Watermarking: When you share your submission externally (to Instagram Stories, Snapchat, TikTok, or other apps), the App adds a watermark containing the Huzzah logo, your username, and the prompt text to the exported image. This watermark is applied locally on your device and only to the exported copy.

2. How We Use Your Information

Providing and Maintaining the App: We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the Huzzah App, including creating and managing your account, processing your submissions, delivering messages, and providing customer support.

Personalization: We use your information to personalize your experience, including ranking and ordering content in your feed based on your social connections, engagement patterns, geographic proximity, and content preferences.

Social Features: We use your information to enable social features including friend discovery, friend suggestions, follow notifications, and messaging.

Safety and Security: We use your information to detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, spam, security breaches, and violations of our Terms of Service.

Communications: We use your information to send you local notifications including daily prompt reminders, streak reminders, alerts when contacts join Huzzah, and birthday greetings. You can control notification preferences in Settings.

Advertising: For non-Pro users, we display advertisements using Google AdMob. Ad serving may involve the collection of device identifiers and usage data by Google. Huzzah Pro subscribers do not see advertisements.

3. How We Share Your Information

With Other Users: Your profile information (username, display name, avatar, bio) is visible to other authenticated users unless you have enabled Private Account mode.

With Service Providers: We share your information with Supabase (backend infrastructure, US-East-2), Google AdMob (advertising for non-Pro users), and Apple (authentication, purchases, music integration).

We Do Not Sell Your Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers in US-East-2 (Ohio, United States). All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and at rest using AES-256 encryption.

We implement Row Level Security (RLS) on all database tables, rate limiting on all write operations, server-side content filtering, secure phone-based OTP authentication, encrypted media storage, automated threat detection, and input sanitization.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active. You may delete your account at any time through Settings > Other > Delete Account. When you delete your account, we permanently delete all your data including your profile, submissions, messages, engagement data, relationships, and conversation history. Account deletion is irreversible and typically completes within 24 hours.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: View your data directly within the App
  • Correction: Update your information through Edit Profile
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data via Settings
  • Consent Withdrawal: Revoke contacts, location, music, notifications, and ad tracking permissions in iOS Settings
  • Privacy Controls: Private Account, Hide Friends & Followers, Message Privacy settings

EU/EEA residents have additional rights under GDPR. California residents have additional rights under CCPA. Contact huzzahapp@yahoo.com to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

Huzzah is not intended for children under 13. We require date of birth verification during account creation. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover an underage account, we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

Email: huzzahapp@yahoo.com

For data deletion requests, use Settings > Other > Delete Account or email us.
